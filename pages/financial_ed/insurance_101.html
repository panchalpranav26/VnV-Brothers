Insurance 101

Understanding insurance is a cornerstone of financial literacy. It’s how families protect what matters most — income, health, and peace of mind.

What Is Insurance?

Insurance is a financial agreement that protects you from major losses by transferring risk to an insurance provider. You pay a small amount called a premium, and in return, the insurer helps cover unexpected events like illness, accidents, or property damage.

[1]

Why It Matters

  • Protects your income and family from financial hardship
  • Helps build resilience against medical or property emergencies
  • Supports long-term wealth strategies and estate planning

V & V Brothers LLC believes that insurance isn’t just a policy — it’s a promise of protection that supports your broader goals for financial wellness and generational wealth.

Core Types of Insurance

Type What It Covers
Life Insurance Provides financial support to your loved ones in the event of your passing.
Health Insurance Reduces the cost of medical care and protects against large hospital bills.
Homeowners / Renters Protects your property, belongings, and liability from damage or theft.
Auto Insurance Covers damage or liability from vehicle accidents.

Choosing the Right Policy

Compare coverage options, read policy terms carefully, and work with a trusted, licensed agent. Focus on:

  • Coverage amount and exclusions
  • Premium vs. deductible trade-offs
  • Company reputation and customer service
